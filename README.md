# ten-hearts-to-unlock
A cute, cozy, pink-coquette gauntlet of 10 tiny terminal games. Win all 10 in a row to unlock the secret prize at the end. Lose even one, and it resets back to Game 1. No mercy, only cuteness. 🩷

DA GAMEZ:

🎮 Tic Tac Toe Showdown — best 2 out of 3 rounds vs an AI
🔢 Guess My Number — a number from 1–10, 3 guesses
💌 Love Wordle — guess a custom word, 2 hints allowed
🔤 Scrambled Secret — unscramble a custom word, 3 hints
✂️ Rock Paper Scissors Hearts — best of 3 vs an AI
🌙 Emoji Riddle Garden — guess the phrase from emoji clues
💐 Memory Bouquet — memorize and repeat a flower sequence
🎭 Two Truths and a Lie — spot the lie
🥀 Wilting Rose Hangman — classic hangman, but the rose wilts instead of a stick figure
💭 How Well Do You Know Me? — a final 3-question trivia round


Win all 10 without ever losing a single one, and the game reveals... a phone number. 😉

how to run:
pip install -r requirements.txt
python3 ten_hearts_to_unlock.py
